Insights for Canada's Digital
Economy Labour Market

The global economy is being transformed under the pressures of health emergencies, trade dynamics, climate change, the digitization of the economy, among other factors - and entire sectors must adapt.

Sustainable pathways for digital employment and skills development are crucial to Canada's future. ICTC's proven expertise in labour-market research into quickly evolving digital technologies is the genesis of our innovative work-integrated-learning programs, online tools for digital competency skills, upskilling initiatives.
